Privacy Policy

Chameleon AI · Effective May 2026

Overview

Chameleon AI is an image enhancement tool developed and operated by CristaLabs. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service at cristalabs.com/tools/chameleonai.

By using Chameleon AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy. We are committed to keeping your data minimal, secure, and never selling it to third parties.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information — Name and email address when you sign up via CristaLabs.
  • Images You Upload — Images submitted for enhancement are processed temporarily and are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data — Basic logs such as timestamp, enhancement level selected, and credit usage for service functionality.
  • Payment Information — Handled entirely by our payment processor (Stripe). We never see or store your card details.
  • Device & Browser Info — Standard technical data (browser type, OS) for performance and security purposes.

Image Processing & Storage

Your privacy with respect to images is our priority. Here is exactly what happens to your images:

  • Images are sent securely over HTTPS to our AI processing endpoint.
  • Processing happens in an isolated, temporary environment (RunPod Serverless).
  • Images are automatically deleted from processing servers immediately after enhancement is complete.
  • We do not use your images for training AI models.
  • We do not share your images with any third party.
Your images are never stored permanently. They exist only during active processing.

Credits & Payments

  • Free Credits — Every registered user receives 3 free credits daily, resetting at midnight UTC.
  • Purchased Credits — Additional credits can be purchased starting from $3. Credits do not expire.
  • Payment Processing — All payments are securely handled by Stripe. CristaLabs does not store payment card data.
  • No Refunds — All credit purchases are final and non-refundable. Please review your purchase before completing payment.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Chameleon AI enhancement service.
  • To manage your account, credits, and purchase history.
  • To send essential service notifications (e.g. low credit alerts) — no marketing spam.
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse of the free credit system.
  • To comply with legal obligations where required.

Third-Party Services

Chameleon AI uses the following trusted third-party services to operate:

  • RunPod — GPU cloud infrastructure for AI image processing. Images are processed and immediately discarded.
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel — Website hosting for cristalabs.com.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with advertisers or data brokers.
